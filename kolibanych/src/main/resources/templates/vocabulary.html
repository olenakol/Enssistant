Read the instructions

In our dictionary there are words that are developed for the study of widely used words of English, for the development of the vocabulary of students from the elementary to advanced level. for convenience, the words are divided into sections that present 20 commonly used English words (word, meaning, example of use in the phrase), followed by exercises to verify the understanding of the meaning and use of words, followed by a text in which all the 20 presented in the beginning section of the new words, then exercises to understand the text. All the presented words (with meanings and examples of use) and texts are sounded. Thus, in the 4000 Series Essential English Words, there are, strictly speaking, 3600 required English words.

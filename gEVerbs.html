Incorrect verbs in English - are the following verbs in which the form Past Simple and Past Participle are NOT formed according to the classical formula: addition to the infinitive (without particle to) ending -ed. Creating the wrong verbs can not be classified or grouped according to certain rules. They need to learn or use the Table of Wrong Verbs/ Irregular verbs .

 

Let's now look at the structure of the Table of Wrong Verbs:

1 column – Infinitive – This is an infinitive, that is, an undefined form of the verb (initial); 2 column – Past Simple – It is a form of past simple (undetected) time; 3 column – Past Participle - this form is used for the formation of a fulfilled time, a participle or a passive state; 4 column - this is, of course, Translation an undefined verb form (infinitive).

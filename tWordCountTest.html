A few words about this test

  • The dictionary stock is divided into two types: active and passive. Active vocabulary is the words that people regularly use in oral speech and writing. Passive vocabulary is a set of words that a person knows and understands on hearing or reading.
  • This test will help you evaluate your passive vocabulary.
  • A bit of statistics to understand the test results. Passive vocabulary of the native language of an educated person, according to various estimates, is not less than 50 - 60 thousand words.
  • Maximum possible test results English languages - 50,000 words.

  • Check the box for each word you know at least one definition for.

    (Don't check boxes for words you know you've seen before, but whose meaning you aren't exactly sure of.)
